Catacumba

Unity

Catacumba is a beat-em-up with RPG aspects that was developed in Unity with premade Assets. I implemented most of the mechanics, a rule-based level generation algorithm with different types of agents that dig corridors and rooms and a custom render pipeline from scratch.

Level Generation

Levels are generated through an agent-based system. Initially, several "walkers" are spawned and they wander through the level leaving a trail, that are the corridors of the dungeon. After a random number of steps, they explode into rooms of different sizes.
